Salmon Sushi Roll

Description:

A stable and classic already in America. This sushi roll is a casual but very satisfying dish. At the center we have some crab mix that is combined with some cream cheese and raw tuna, rolled into it's classic circular shape topped with raw salmon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rushi rice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 sheet nori seaweed
  • 1 1/2 ounces smoked salmon
  • small cucumber
  • 2 tablespoon cream cheese
  • thin slices of salmon sashimi

Instructions:

  1. Put rice in a bowl and fill with water you will notice the water is cloudy. Drain water out and repeat the process until the water is clear.
  2. Bring 1 cup of rice and 1 cup of water to a boil.
  3. Reduce to a simmer and cover.
  4. Cook about 15minutes. Just until all the water is adsorbed, could be more or less time. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. Work vinegar through rice by pushing around with a spoon, also start fanning your rice. You want the rice to cool as quickly as possible.
  6. Spread out 3/4 to 1 cup of cooked rice per sheet of Nori (seaweed paper). Use wet hands and spread around the whole sheet leaving a 1 inch border on the bottom.
  7. 1/3 of the way up from the bottom smear a line of cream cheese.
  8. Place Salmon on top of the cream cheese.

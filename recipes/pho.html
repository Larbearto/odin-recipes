Vietnamese Pho

Vietnamese Pho

Description:

This Vietnamese dish from Vietnam is the definition of comfort food. There is nothing like a warm bowl of rice noodles steeped in a broth slowly cooked for 24 hours. Make sure to enjoy with some friends!

Ingredients:

  • Rice Noodles
  • Chicken or Beef Broth
  • Protein of choice (beef slices or chicken)
  • Onions
  • Green Onions
  • Bag of Pho Flavor mix

Instructions:

  1. Boil a large bowl of Water
  2. Pour contents of Pho Flavor packet into boiling water
  3. Blanch Rice Pho Noodles until texture is soft but still firm
  4. Combine all other ingredients together
  5. Pour pho broth into bowl with ingredients and enjoy!

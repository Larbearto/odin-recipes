Chinese Egg Rolls

Description:

Delicous combination of soft vegetable and pork mix wrapped in a crunchy fried wonton wrapper.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder, or more to taste
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 2 ounces shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 quart peanut oil for frying, or as needed
  • 8 (7 inch square) egg roll wrappers
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds (Optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil a large bowl of Water
  2. Pour contents of Pho Flavor packet into boiling water
  3. Blanch Rice Pho Noodles until texture is soft but still firm
  4. Combine all other ingredients together
  5. Pour pho broth into bowl with ingredients and enjoy!
